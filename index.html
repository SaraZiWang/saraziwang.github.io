⚙️ Mechanical Design
Intelligent System & Structural Design
From aeroelastic simulation of novel wing structures to AI-assisted mechanical design. Combining solid mechanics foundations with modern ML methods to push design capability further and faster.
🕸️ Knowledge & Data
Knowledge Graph & Data Interoperability
Knowledge-graph-based capability representation for manufacturing system design. Breaking data silos and integrating multi-disciplinary expertise across metrology, robotics, and production engineering.
Case Study · Aerospace
Airbus RACER – Box-Wing Aeroelastic Design
Developed the first aeroelastic evaluation algorithms for the novel RACER box-wing structure. Proposed a reconfigurable tooling strategy that resolved tolerance, stress, and cost challenges — contributing to the maiden flight in April 2024.
→ 420 km/h cruising speed — 3.2% above target
🔷 Digital Twin
Factory Digital Twin
Real-time digital-physical alignment using photogrammetry and point cloud analysis. Enabling 3D layout optimisation and continuous monitoring across reconfigurable manufacturing cells.
Case Study · Airbus
Wingbox – Supervisory Visualisation & Control
Designed the real-time supervisory feedback system for fully automated wingbox assembly — rib positioning, riveting, sealing. Successfully deployed at Airbus, integrating multiple systems into a centralised human interface platform.
→ Live deployment at Airbus production facility
🏭 Manufacturing
Automated Assembly Systems
Design and prototype development of reconfigurable, multi-product smart assembly systems. Demonstrated 50-micron repeatability and sub-15-minute changeover in aerospace applications — outperforming any known state-of-the-art system.
Case Study · GKN Aerospace
Smart Assembly Demonstrator
Led technology selection and commissioning for a multi-product smart assembly system. GKN built an in-house replica for further maturity and adoption. Technology progressed from TRL2 to TRL5.
→ 45%+ projected cost saving · 50μm repeatability · <15 min changeover
Case Study · Airbus RACER
Reconfigurable Tooling for Box-Wing Assembly
Proposed a reconfigurable tooling strategy for the RACER demonstrator — designed to resolve tolerance, stress, and cost challenges unique to the novel box-wing structure.
→ Tooling strategy adopted for Airbus RACER demonstrator
⚡ AI & Optimisation
Dynamic Layout Optimisation
Reinforcement learning, metaheuristics, and multi-agent swarm learning applied to dynamic 3D layout. Finds optimal configurations to minimise production time, while enhancing safety and quality .
Case Study · Journal of Manufacturing Systems · 2024
Digital Twin-Based Photogrammetry Layout Optimisation for Reconfigurable Manufacturing
Developed a digital twin framework that evaluates photogrammetry camera field-of-view coverage and automatically optimises 3D camera layouts within reconfigurable manufacturing cells. Combining simulation with optimisation algorithms to configure metrology setups across changing production environments — eliminating manual trial-and-error placement.
♻️ Sustainability
Design for Circularity
Incorporating circularity, reuse, and disassembly as first-class design goals. Developing frameworks for reconfigurable manufacturing systems that support end-of-life recovery and remanufacturing.
